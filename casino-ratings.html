What We Rate On

Here's what separates a great sweepstakes casino from a mediocre one. These are the categories our community weighs when casting their votes.

Daily Login Value

How much free Sweeps Coins do you get just for showing up every day? The best casinos give $0.50–$1+ in SC daily without spending a dime. This is the bread and butter of the daily collection hustle.

Welcome Bonus

The no-deposit welcome offer matters. Top casinos hand you 5–25 SC just for signing up — no credit card, no purchase required. That's free money to start playing with day one.

Game Selection

Variety and quality. Does the casino have 5,000 games or 50? Are they running popular providers like Pragmatic Play and BGaming, or just generic slots? More games = more chances to win.

Payout Speed

How fast do you get your money after redeeming? Some casinos pay within 24 hours. Others make you wait 7–10 business days. Speed matters when real cash is on the line.

Minimum Redemption

How much SC do you need before you can cash out? Some casinos let you redeem at $5–$10. Others make you accumulate $50–$100 first. Lower minimums = faster access to your earnings.

Trustworthiness

Parent company reputation, how long they've been around, real user reviews, and payout consistency. We don't rate casinos that have a history of ghosting players or delaying withdrawals.
