RTP Games & Strategy

Most sweepstakes sites require playthrough before redemption, commonly around 1x. This sheet points you toward higher-RTP games designed for preserving earned SC while completing that requirement. The goal is not to chase a huge win; it is to lose the least amount possible on the way to redemption.

1. Know playthrough Most sites make you wager earned SC before redeeming. Always confirm the current playthrough rules first.
2. Use RTP RTP means Return to Player. Higher-RTP games generally give back more over time than lower-RTP games.
3. Preserve SC These games are for completing playthrough with less expected loss, not for trying to hit a jackpot.
4. Counter gamble This strategy is counter gambling: use optimized games to protect as much earned SC as possible.
