What is Fliff?

Fliff is a sweepstakes sports betting platform — no slots, no table games, just sports picks. Launched in 2022, it fills a unique niche for people who want to bet on sports using the sweepstakes model.

The platform is mobile-only (phone app required). You get $1 SC daily plus an additional $0.10 every 2 hours — which can stack up nicely if you check in throughout the day. The $50 minimum redemption and fast payouts make it one of the better earners in the space.

The catch: your daily stops when you hit a $5 balance. You need to place a bet to drop below $5 and restart the earning. This encourages engagement but also means you can't just hoard dailies passively. If you like sports at all, Fliff is worth having on your phone.
