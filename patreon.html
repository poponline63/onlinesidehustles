Not for the Hustler tier. For Automator and Counter Gambler, basic comfort with a terminal is helpful but not required — the video walkthroughs show you every click. The scripts are copy-paste with clear instructions for configuring your credentials.

How much can I realistically earn? ▼

Daily collection alone (free bonuses from 70+ sites): $900-1,200/month. Add churning: +$300-600/month. Add matched betting: +$100-300/month ongoing. Total realistic range after 2-3 months of setup: $1,200-1,500/month. Some members earn more, some earn less. It depends on consistency and how many sites you work.

Is this gambling? ▼

No. Counter gambling specifically exploits promotional bonuses using math. You're not betting and hoping to win — you're calculating expected value and only engaging when the math is positive. It's closer to credit card churning than gambling. The guides explain the distinction in detail.
