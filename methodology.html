Review Methodology

How we rank daily login sites, sweepstakes casinos, tools, and online side hustle opportunities.

Short version: we rank opportunities by practical user value, not by who pays the highest commission. Affiliate relationships may support the site, but rankings should still reflect bonus value, reliability, redemption friction, risk, user experience, and community feedback.

What We Evaluate

For sweepstakes-style daily login sites and casinos, we look at the factors that affect a real user trying to build a repeatable routine:

  • Daily bonus value and claim consistency.
  • Welcome package quality and whether the value is realistic after playthrough.
  • Minimum redemption amount and payout methods.
  • Known state restrictions and eligibility limitations.
  • KYC difficulty, support responsiveness, and redemption delay reports.
  • Game selection, RTP options, mobile experience, and ease of daily use.
  • Terms changes, promo bans, shutdown risk, and community-reported red flags.

How Rankings Work

Rankings are based on a mix of site data, hands-on review, public terms, user reports, and repeat-use practicality. A high daily bonus does not automatically make a site a top recommendation if redemption is difficult, terms are unclear, or user reports show recurring payout issues.

Update Process

Offers, redemption rules, state access, and daily rewards can change quickly. We prioritize updates when a site changes its bonus, redemption minimum, KYC process, payout behavior, or terms. Pages may include "last updated" or "last verified" dates where available.

What We Do Not Promise

We do not guarantee earnings, redemptions, approval, availability, or future offer terms. Results vary by state, platform rules, user behavior, verification status, and changing site policies. Treat all earning estimates as educational ranges, not guaranteed income.

How To Report A Change

If you find a changed bonus, broken signup link, payout delay, state restriction, or inaccurate review note, send it through the contact page. Include the site name, date, screenshot if possible, and what changed.
