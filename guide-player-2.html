What is the Player 2 Strategy?

Once you've mastered sweepstakes and maximized your own earning potential, you can multiply your income by utilizing the "Player 2" strategy. Help your friends and family earn passive income while you manage their accounts — typically splitting profits 50/50. The more people you help, the more you earn.

Since each person can only have one account per site, every new "Player 2" effectively doubles your earning potential on that site. With 5 people, you're earning 5x. With 10, you're earning 10x. This legitimate strategy lets you leverage your expertise while providing real value to others who don't have the time or knowledge to maximize their own earnings.

How to Set Up Your Player 2 Network

1

Create Your Main Account

Set up your primary accounts on Skrill and other platforms using your personal information. This will be your hub for managing multiple income streams and transferring funds between you and your Player 2s.

2

Have Your Player 2 Create Their Accounts

Your friend or family member creates their own accounts using their real information. Everything must be in their name for legal compliance — this is crucial for avoiding any issues with the platforms.

3

Fund Their Accounts

Send money from your main account to your Player 2's account via Skrill or other payment methods. This creates a clean, traceable transaction history for tax purposes and keeps everything above board.

4

Sign Up & Manage Their Sweepstakes

Use your expertise to sign them up for sweepstakes sites and manage their accounts. Collect daily bonuses, enter giveaways, and optimize their earnings. Your Player 2 doesn't have to do anything — it's completely passive for them.

5

Split the Profits

Typically a 50/50 split — they get passive income for providing their identity, you get rewarded for your expertise and time. Everyone wins, and the arrangement scales as you add more Player 2s.

⚠️ Important Requirements & Best Practices

🏠

Household Limits

Maximum of 2 sweepstakes users per household. Each account must use separate devices. Player 2s in different households are ideal for maximizing opportunities.

💻

Remote Access Setup

For Player 2s in different locations, use remote access software like HelpWire (unlimited free access) or set up an affordable dedicated PC solely for remote management.

📊

Tax Compliance

Your Player 2 is responsible for taxes on their own earnings. However, the income they generate will far exceed any tax obligations — it's still highly profitable for them even after tax.

🔄

Scalability

The more people you know, the more you can earn. Build a network of Player 2s across different households to keep maximizing income potential without hitting per-household limits.

🖥️

Remote Hardware Setup

For remote Player 2 management, get an affordable PC bundle (includes keyboard & mouse — monitor needed for initial setup only). After setup, use a headless display adapter to emulate a monitor for remote access.

🔒

Legal Compliance

All accounts must be in the actual person's name with their real information. Never create fake accounts or use false information — this strategy only works when done legitimately and with people who trust you.
