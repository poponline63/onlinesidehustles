How to Set Up Your Player 2 Network

1

Create Your Main Account

Set up your primary accounts on Skrill and other platforms using your personal information. This will be your hub for managing multiple income streams and transferring funds.

2

Have Your Player 2 Create Their Accounts

Your friend or family member creates their own accounts using their information. Everything must be in their name for legal compliance - this is crucial for avoiding any issues.

3

Fund Their Accounts

Send money from your main account to your Player 2's account (via Skrill or other payment methods). This creates a clean, traceable transaction history for tax purposes.

4

Sign Up & Manage Their Sweepstakes

Use your expertise to sign them up for sweepstake websites and manage their accounts. Collect daily bonuses, enter giveaways, and optimize their earnings. Your Player 2 doesn't have to do anything!

5

Split the Profits

Typically a 50/50 split - they get passive income for providing their identity, you get rewarded for your expertise and time. Everyone wins!
