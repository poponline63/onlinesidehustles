💡 What is the Player 2 Strategy?

Once you've mastered sweepstakes and maximized your own earning potential, you can multiply your income using the Player 2 strategy. You help friends and family set up their own sweepstakes accounts and manage them on their behalf — doing the daily logins, collecting bonuses, and optimizing their earnings. In return, you split the profits 50/50.

Since each person can only hold one account per site, every new Player 2 directly doubles your earning potential on that site. With 5 Player 2s you're earning 5x. With 10, you're earning 10x. This is a legitimate, scalable strategy that lets you leverage your expertise while giving others a hands-free passive income stream.

🚀 How to Set Up Your Player 2 Network

1

Create Your Main Account

Set up your primary accounts on Skrill and sweepstakes platforms using your own personal information. This becomes your hub for managing income streams and transferring funds between you and all your Player 2s.

2

Have Your Player 2 Create Their Accounts

Your friend or family member creates their own sweepstakes and Skrill accounts using their real information — real name, address, and SSN for KYC. Everything must be in their name for legal compliance. This is non-negotiable.

3

Fund Their Accounts

Send funds from your main account to your Player 2 via Skrill or another payment method so they can purchase welcome packages. This creates a clean, traceable transaction history that's important for tax reporting.

4

Sign Up & Manage Their Sweepstakes

Use your expertise to register them across sweepstakes sites, collect daily bonuses, complete welcome packages, and optimize their earnings. Your Player 2 doesn't have to do a single thing — it's 100% passive for them.

5

Split the Profits 50/50

When it's time to cash out, earnings are redeemed through their verified account. They transfer your half to you via Skrill or bank transfer. You get paid for your expertise, they get paid for doing nothing. Everyone wins.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Player 2 strategy legal?
▼

Yes. Each account belongs to a real person using their real information. You are managing accounts on their behalf with their full knowledge and consent — this is completely legal. What is not allowed is creating fake accounts or using someone else's identity without their knowledge.

How do I split profits with my Player 2?
▼

Most people use a 50/50 split. Your Player 2 redeems their earnings to their bank account or Skrill, then transfers your half to you. Some operators use 60/40 or 70/30 splits depending on the arrangement. Get the agreement in writing — even a text message — to protect both parties.

How many Player 2s can I manage at once?
▼

There is no hard limit set by the sweepstakes sites — the limit is your time and logistics. Most successful operators manage 5-10 Player 2 accounts. Beyond that, you'll want automation tools like Sweeps App to handle daily collections, otherwise it becomes unmanageable.

Do I need to be in the same location as my Player 2?
▼

No. Remote Player 2s are actually preferable since they're in a different household with separate IP addresses, which reduces risk of account linking. Use HelpWire for free remote PC access or set up a small dedicated PC at their location that you manage remotely.

Who is responsible for taxes?
▼

Your Player 2 is responsible for reporting and paying taxes on their portion since the accounts are registered in their name. You are responsible for reporting any income you receive from the arrangement. Consult a tax professional if you're unsure — but the income far exceeds any tax burden for both parties.

What if my Player 2 wants to stop?
▼

Since the accounts are in their name, they can close them at any time. This is why having a written agreement upfront is important — it sets expectations clearly. In practice, most Player 2s don't want to stop once they see consistent passive income hitting their account every month.
