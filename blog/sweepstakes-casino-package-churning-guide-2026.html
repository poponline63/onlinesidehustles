What Is Package Churning?

Package churning is the practice of purchasing sweepstakes casino bonus packages, playing through the Sweeps Coins (SC) on high Return-to-Player (RTP) games, and then withdrawing the remaining balance as real cash.

Sweepstakes casinos sell packages where you receive more SC than the dollar value you spent. This incentive exists to get players spending money on their platform. The churning strategy flips this — instead of gambling away your coins, you systematically play through them with the best odds possible and pocket the profit.

💡 Simple Example

You buy a $50 package that gives you 75 SC. You play through all 75 SC on Auto Roulette (97.4% RTP). You end up with approximately 73 SC. You withdraw $73. Net profit: ~$23 on a $50 investment.

How Sweepstakes Packages Work

Every sweepstakes casino offers packages for purchase. The key numbers to look at are:

  • SC value received vs. purchase price — a $10 package giving 18 SC is better than a $10 package giving 12 SC
  • Bonus percentage — welcome packages often give 100–250% extra SC on top of the base value
  • Minimum redemption — most sites require 50–100 SC to be eligible to withdraw
  • Playthrough requirement — almost always 1x (you wager your total balance once before withdrawing)

Welcome packages are always your best opportunity — they offer the highest SC-to-dollar ratio you'll ever see on any site. After the welcome package, sites continue to offer regular daily, happy hour, and promotional packages that keep the income stream going.

Understanding Playthrough

Playthrough (also called a "wash") is the requirement to wager your SC balance before you can withdraw. On 99% of sweepstakes sites, this is a 1x playthrough requirement.

What this means in practice: if you have 100 SC, you must place bets totaling at least 100 SC before you can cash out. You don't lose 100 SC — the game returns most of it based on its RTP rate. A 97% RTP game returns approximately 97 SC for every 100 SC wagered on average.

🎯 Starting Balance Strategy

If the minimum withdrawal is 100 SC, start your playthrough with 110–130 SC. This buffer accounts for normal variance. If you run slightly unlucky during playthrough, you'll still land above the minimum and be eligible to withdraw.

The critical rule: once playthrough is complete, withdraw immediately. Do not continue playing. Every SC you play beyond the requirement is money you could have cashed out.

⚠️ Avoid Low-RTP Slots for Playthrough

Many slots on sweepstakes sites have RTPs of 88–93%. Never use these for playthrough — you'll lose significantly more SC before you're eligible to withdraw. Always confirm the RTP in the game info before playing.

Step-by-Step Churning Strategy

Step 1 — Prioritize Welcome Packages

Welcome packages are the single best deal you'll ever get on any site. They give the highest SC-per-dollar ratio and are available to every new player. Work through God Tier and High Tier sites first — these are ranked by overall quality and package value in our spreadsheet.

Step 2 — Check the RTP Game Before Purchasing

Before buying a package on any site, confirm which high-RTP game is available there. If the site only has games below 94% RTP, it may not be worth churning — save your bankroll for better sites.

Step 3 — Use Small Bet Sizes

Bet the minimum allowed amount per spin or hand. The more individual bets you place, the closer your actual results will track to the stated RTP. Large bets introduce more variance — you could win big or lose everything in a few spins. Small bets keep results consistent.

Step 4 — Complete the Full Playthrough, Then Withdraw

Play until you've wagered your full SC balance. Most sites have a playthrough tracker visible in your account dashboard. Once done — withdraw. Bank transfers take 1–5 business days. Skrill is faster (1–3 days) and available on many sites.

Step 5 — Move to Ongoing Packages

After welcome packages are exhausted, sites still offer regular promotional packages. WOW Vegas, Stake.us, McLuck, and Fortune Coins all have consistent happy hour and daily packages worth churning on an ongoing basis.
