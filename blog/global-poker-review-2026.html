🎮 Poker Games Available

Texas Hold'em

The dominant format. Cash games run around the clock at stakes from $0.01/$0.02 SC up to $1/$2 SC. 6-max and full ring tables available. Traffic is highest in evenings and weekends US time.

Omaha (PLO)

Pot-Limit Omaha is the second most popular variant. Cash game traffic is thinner than Hold'em but tables run regularly. PLO-specific tournaments run weekly. Ideal for players who prefer the complexity of four-card hands.

Short Deck (6+)

Short Deck Hold'em (cards 2-5 removed) is a newer offering. Traffic is lightest here — expect to wait for a table during off-peak hours. Best played during weekend peak times.

Tournaments

Global Poker runs a daily tournament schedule including sit-and-gos (SNGs), multi-table tournaments (MTTs), and guaranteed prize pool events. The Sunday Major is the flagship event with a large SC guaranteed prize pool. Satellite qualifiers for the Sunday Major run throughout the week.
