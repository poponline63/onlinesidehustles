1. Cashback Apps — Get Paid for Shopping You're Already Doing

These apps give you money back on purchases you'd make anyway. The key is having all of them active simultaneously so every purchase earns from multiple sources.

Rakuten — Best Overall Cashback App

Rakuten is the gold standard of cashback apps. Install the browser extension and it automatically activates cashback whenever you shop at 3,500+ stores — Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, and hundreds more. Rates range from 1% to 15%+ depending on the store and current promotion.

  • Sign-up bonus: $30 when you spend $30 within 90 days
  • Payout: Quarterly via PayPal or check
  • Best for: Online shoppers who order frequently — it's completely automatic once installed

Ibotta — Best for Grocery Cashback

Scan your grocery receipt and earn cash back on specific products — anywhere from $0.25 to $5 per item. Works at virtually every major grocery chain. The key is to browse the available offers BEFORE you shop and adjust your list accordingly.

  • Sign-up bonus: $20 on your first receipt submission
  • Payout: PayPal or Venmo, $20 minimum
  • Best for: Families who spend $200+ on groceries monthly — the offers stack up fast

Fetch Rewards — Easiest Receipt App

Unlike Ibotta, Fetch doesn't require you to activate specific offers. Scan any grocery, restaurant, or gas station receipt and earn points automatically. 5,000 points = $5. The simplicity is its strength — just scan everything.

  • Sign-up bonus: Points for first receipt
  • Payout: Gift cards, $3 minimum
  • Best for: Everyone — there's no reason not to use this alongside Ibotta on every receipt

2. Survey & Task Apps — Earn for Completing Offers

GPT (Get-Paid-To) sites pay you to complete surveys, try out apps, sign up for services, and watch videos. FreeCash is in a different league from the rest — prioritize it.

FreeCash — Highest Paying GPT Platform

FreeCash is consistently the top-rated GPT platform in 2026. Offers pay $2–$30+ each, you can cash out instantly to PayPal or crypto with no minimum, and the earning potential is significantly higher than Swagbucks or InboxDollars.

  • Best offers: App installs, casino sign-ups, survey packs
  • Payout: Instant — PayPal, Skrill, crypto, gift cards
  • Monthly potential: $30–$100+ depending on offer availability in your region

Swagbucks — Best Passive Earning GPT

Swagbucks earns slower per hour than FreeCash, but has more passive earning options — you can earn SB just by having their search engine run searches in the background, watching video playlists, and shopping through their portal.

  • Sign-up bonus: $10
  • Best for: Passive video/search earnings alongside other work — run it in a background tab
  • Payout: PayPal or gift cards, $3 minimum

InboxDollars — Simple Passive Video Income

Get paid to read emails, watch video clips, and take surveys. $5 sign-up bonus. Lower earning rates than FreeCash but the passive video earning (auto-play playlists) adds up with literally zero effort.

3. Passive Phone Apps — Earn While You Live Your Life

These require zero active effort after setup. They run in the background while you live your life.

Coin App

Leave it running as you go about your day and it earns COIN tokens based on your movement and location. Commuters and people who walk a lot earn the most. Redeem for gift cards or PayPal.

Mode Mobile

Earn points for everyday phone actions — charging your phone, listening to music, locking your screen. Genuinely passive. Redeem for cash or gift cards.

Honeygain

Share your unused internet bandwidth in the background. Set it up once and forget it. Works best on a device that's always connected to WiFi (spare laptop, tablet, etc.). Completely passive — just let it run.

5. Combining with Sweepstakes Casinos

If you're already doing sweepstakes daily collections, reward apps fit into the exact same routine:

  1. Open FreeCash — complete one or two offers (5–10 minutes)
  2. Collect your sweepstakes daily bonuses (5–10 minutes)
  3. Open Swagbucks — start a video playlist in the background
  4. Go about your day — Coin app and Honeygain run passively
  5. Evening: scan any receipts from the day into Ibotta and Fetch

Total active time: about 15–20 minutes per day. Everything else is passive.

6. Tips for Maximizing Every App

  • Stack receipt apps: Scan every receipt in BOTH Ibotta AND Fetch — there's no rule against using both on the same receipt
  • Activate Rakuten before every online purchase — even if you forgot, check if the browser extension has a pending activation
  • Check FreeCash daily — new high-paying offers appear regularly. The best ones get taken by early birds
  • Run Swagbucks video playlists on a second screen — you don't have to watch, just let them play while you work
  • Use sign-up bonuses strategically — save the apps with the best sign-up bonuses until you have a qualifying purchase ready (e.g., Rakuten's $30 bonus requires a $30 purchase within 90 days)
  • Refer friends — most of these apps have referral bonuses worth $5–$30 per person you bring in
