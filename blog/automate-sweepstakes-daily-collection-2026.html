The Problem with Manual Collection

At its core, the Daily Dollar Method is simple: log in to each site daily, claim your SC, repeat. But with 87+ verified sites in our spreadsheet, doing this manually means:

  • 1–3 minutes per site × 87 sites = 1.5–4 hours of daily clicking
  • Missing any site on any day breaks your streak and costs bonus SC
  • Travel, illness, or a busy day means missed income permanently
  • Human error — forgetting login credentials, missing a claim window

This is why automation isn't just convenient — it's essential for anyone serious about maximizing their sweepstakes income.
