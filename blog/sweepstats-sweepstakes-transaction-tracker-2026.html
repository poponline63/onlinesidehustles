SweepStats: The Free Sweepstakes Transaction Tracker You Actually Need

If you're running daily collections across dozens of casinos, you've probably outgrown your spreadsheet. Here's the tool that fixes that.

The SweepStats dashboard — deposits, redemptions, profit, and charts all in one view.

Let's be real — if you're running 30+ daily collection sites, your Google Sheet is probably a mess. Multiple tabs, manual entry, formulas breaking, no idea which casino is actually profitable vs. which one is bleeding money. We've all been there.

That's why when our community member @labago dropped SweepStats in the Discord, people noticed. He's a web developer who also grinds daily collections, so he built exactly the tool he wished existed.

And it's completely free.

What SweepStats Actually Does

It's a sweepstakes transaction tracker — purpose-built for what we do. Not some generic finance app someone slapped a casino label on. Here's what you get:

  • Dashboard — Total deposits, redemptions, profit, pending payouts, transaction count, active casinos. Cumulative profit chart. Per-casino profit breakdown. All on one screen.
  • Casino Management — Every casino gets its own card with profit, ROI, deposits, redemptions, and pending status. Sort by profit, deposits, or alphabetically.
  • Transaction Log — Full history with filters for casino, type (deposit/redemption), date range, and status. Color-coded so you can scan fast.
  • CSV Import — Already have a spreadsheet? Export it as CSV, upload, map your columns, done. Bulk import with validation.

Per-casino tracking with profit, ROI, and pending redemptions at a glance.

Why It Beats a Spreadsheet

Look, spreadsheets work when you're tracking 5 casinos. Once you hit 20, 30, 50+ sites? Forget it. Here's what SweepStats gives you that Sheets doesn't:

  • Instant ROI per casino — no formulas to maintain
  • Pending redemption tracking — know exactly what's coming in
  • Visual profit trends — see your growth over time without building charts
  • Filtering that works — by casino, date, type, status
  • Mobile-friendly — check your numbers from your phone

Transaction log with filters — deposits in red, redemptions in green, pending flagged.

Getting Started

It takes about 2 minutes:

  • Go to sweepstats.com
  • Create an account (free)
  • Add your casinos and start logging transactions
  • Or just import your existing spreadsheet via CSV

CSV import — bring your existing spreadsheet data over in one upload.

If you've got a spreadsheet going, the CSV import is honestly the move. Export, upload, map columns, validate, import. Your entire history transfers over.
