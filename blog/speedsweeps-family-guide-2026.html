🎦 Playthrough Rules Explained

All SpeedSweeps family sites apply a slot-only playthrough requirement to bonus sweeps coins. This is a standard industry practice but one that catches new players off guard. Here is what it means in practice:

  • Bonus sweeps coins must be played through a certain number of times on eligible slot games before they can be redeemed for cash or crypto.
  • Table games (blackjack, roulette, baccarat) typically do not count toward playthrough on bonus coins.
  • Sweeps coins you receive from purchase packages or direct promotions may have different playthrough terms than free daily bonus coins — read each site's terms carefully.
  • Coins you win while playing with already-cleared sweeps coins are generally free to redeem without additional playthrough.

The practical advice: use your bonus coins on slot games you actually enjoy. RichSweeps has the widest game selection for this purpose, making playthrough completion less of a chore.
