# Pinterest Marketing Guide for OnlineSidehustles.info

## Setup Steps

### 1. Create a Pinterest Business Account
- Go to business.pinterest.com
- Sign up with your site email
- Select "Online Education" or "Finance" as your business type
- Claim your website: Add the meta tag `<meta name="p:domain_verify" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE"/>` to your site's `<head>` on every page (or use the HTML file verification method)

### 2. Enable Rich Pins
- Add Open Graph meta tags to every page (already done on most pages)
- Use Pinterest's Rich Pin Validator: https://developers.pinterest.com/tools/url-debugger/
- Rich Pins pull your article title, description, and author automatically

## Posting Schedule

### Week 1-4 (Launch Phase)
- **Pin 2-3x per day** (spread throughout the day)
- Pin each major guide page
- Create 2-3 variations of each pin (different images/descriptions)
- Join relevant group boards if available

### Ongoing Schedule
- **Pin 1-2x per day** consistently
- Re-pin older content monthly with updated descriptions
- Pin new blog posts on publish day
- Share seasonal/timely content (e.g., "Best side hustles for summer 2026")

### Best Times to Pin
- **Weekdays:** 8-11 PM EST (peak Pinterest usage)
- **Weekends:** 2-4 PM EST
- **Best days:** Saturday and Sunday for lifestyle/money content

## Pin Image Best Practices

- **Size:** 1000 x 1500px (2:3 ratio) — Pinterest's preferred format
- **Colors:** Use the site's green/gold palette (#32CD32, #FFD700, #0a1f0a)
- **Text overlay:** Bold headline text on each pin (readable on mobile)
- **Branding:** Include "OnlineSidehustles.info" logo or URL on every pin
- **Template:** Create 2-3 pin templates in Canva matching the site aesthetic
