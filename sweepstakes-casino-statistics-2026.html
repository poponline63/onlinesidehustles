Market Size & Revenue

  • The global social casino market is valued at roughly $10.08 billion in 2026, up from about $9.24 billion in 2025, growing at a ~9% compound annual growth rate. (The Business Research Company)
  • Analysts project the market reaching ~$14.4 billion by 2030 at a ~9.4% CAGR. (The Business Research Company)
  • In the US specifically, sweepstakes-model operators are estimated to drive $12–13 billion in Gold Coin purchases in 2026, with net operator revenue around $3.6–4.2 billion. (industry analyst estimates)
  • US players spent an estimated $8.5–10.6 billion on Gold Coin packages in 2024, illustrating how quickly the vertical has scaled. (RG.org)
  • The American Gaming Association and multiple trade outlets now peg the US sweepstakes segment at roughly $10 billion in annual revenue. (Gaming Innovation / AGA)

Players & Demographics

  • ~38 million Americans are estimated to be active sweepstakes casino users as of 2025. (Racine County Eye trend report)
  • The player base skews young: roughly 58% of users are aged 25–44, a notably younger profile than traditional gambling audiences. (industry surveys)
  • Gender is nearly even — about 51% male, 49% female. (industry surveys)
  • 72.3% of social casino activity happens on mobile devices. (RG.org)
  • Around 42% of users earn under $50,000 per year, underscoring the appeal of the free-to-play / no-purchase-necessary model. (industry surveys)
  • A 2025 American Gaming Association study found that most sweepstakes casino users view the activity as a form of gambling, a key data point in the regulatory debate. (AGA via Yogonet)

How Sweepstakes Casinos Work (Context)

  • Sweepstakes casinos run on a dual-currency model: Gold Coins (for-fun, non-redeemable) and Sweeps Coins (redeemable for cash prizes).
  • They operate under sweepstakes promotional law rather than a gambling license, which is why a no-purchase-necessary (AMOE) path is always required.
  • Most platforms set a minimum redemption between $50 and $100 in Sweeps Coins.
  • Daily login bonuses, the core draw for value-focused players, typically range from $0.30 to several free Sweeps Coins per day across platforms.
