What is SweepStats?

If you're running daily collections across 50+ sites, you know the spreadsheet struggle. Tracking deposits, redemptions, pending payouts, profit per casino — it gets messy fast.

Our community member @labago is a web developer and built SweepStats specifically for what we do here. It's a clean, purpose-built web app that replaces your Google Sheets tracking with something actually designed for sweepstakes casino management.

It's free to use and built by someone who actually grinds daily collections — so it covers exactly what you need.

Features

📊

Full Dashboard

See your deposits, redemptions, and profit at a glance. Charts and per-casino breakdowns all in one place — no more digging through spreadsheet tabs.

Track every casino you're on. See ROI, pending redemptions, sort however you want. Know exactly where your money is at all times.

Full transaction history with filters for casino, date range, status — all of it. Find any transaction instantly instead of scrolling through rows.
