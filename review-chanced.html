What is Chanced?

Chanced (formerly Chanced.com) is run by Gold Coin Group and launched in 2023. It sits in medium tier, which might surprise you given the $1.00 SC daily bonus — but there's a catch that holds it back.

The game library has around 350+ titles with decent variety. The daily bonus reaches $1.00 SC after a 5-day streak, which is competitive. But here's the asterisk: daily SC has a 10x playthrough requirement, compared to the typical 1-3x at other platforms. That means you need to wager your daily SC ten times before it becomes redeemable.

Purchased packages have a more reasonable 3x playthrough rate. Chanced is legit and pays out, but the high playthrough on dailies significantly reduces the effective value of that $1.00 claim.

Chanced requires a 5-day login streak to unlock the full $1.00 SC daily bonus. Here's the important part: daily SC has a 10x playthrough requirement.
