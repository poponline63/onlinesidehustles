What is Tao Fortune?

Tao Fortune is an A1 platform, making it a sister site to FunRize and NoLimitCoins. It has an Asian-inspired theme and launched in 2023. The platform recently fixed redemption issues that had previously earned it bad marks from the community.

The game library has around 300+ titles. Like other A1 sites, you'll see some game overlap with FunRize and NoLimitCoins. The daily bonus is modest at $0.10-$0.30 SC plus free spins, and the popup ads can be annoying.

Tao Fortune is the weakest of the A1 trio in most areas. The 21+ age requirement (compared to 18+ for FunRize and NoLimitCoins), lower welcome offer percentage, and spammy interface hold it back. It works as a rotation filler, but prioritize the other A1 sites first.
