❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an existing eBay account?

Yes, you’ll need an eBay seller account. New accounts work fine, though aged accounts with some feedback history tend to get fewer restrictions. The Infinity onboarding guide walks you through setup from scratch if you’re starting fresh.

Is dropshipping allowed on eBay?

Yes. eBay permits dropshipping as long as you’re the seller of record and items ship within your stated handling time. Infinity handles the order flow compliantly and ensures tracking information is submitted on time.

What is the Posting Service?

For a limited time, Crossroads offers a hands-on setup service at the beginning of your membership. Their team will list products to your store and get it running while you learn the platform. It’s designed to get you earning from day one before you’ve mastered the tool yourself.

Basic gives you 2,000 listings plus the Volume Checker and Automator — ideal for scaling a single store. Pro gives you unlimited listings and support for 2 eBay stores. If you’re serious about building a high-volume operation, Pro pays for itself quickly.

Most members see their first sale within the first 1–2 weeks once listings are live, depending on the products and their eBay account age. With the Posting Service, your store can have products live on day one.
