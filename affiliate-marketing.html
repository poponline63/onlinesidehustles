How Affiliate Marketing Actually Works

It's not complicated. You sign up for an affiliate program, get a unique tracking link, and share it in your content. When someone clicks your link and buys something, you earn a commission Ś usually 3% to 50% of the sale price depending on the program.

Sign Up for Affiliate Programs

Join programs for products you actually use or would recommend. Amazon Associates is the easiest to start with Ś they sell everything, so almost any niche works. Most programs approve you within 24 hours.

Create Content Around Products

Write blog posts, make YouTube videos, post on TikTok, or create Pinterest pins that naturally mention products. "Best budget headphones for 2026" or "My home office setup" Ś content that helps people make buying decisions.

Include Your Affiliate Links

Drop your unique tracking links in the content. Blog post links to products, YouTube description links, TikTok bio links. Always disclose that you earn from recommendations (FTC requires it).

Earn Commissions Passively

Once the content is published, it keeps earning. A blog post from 6 months ago can still generate clicks and sales every single day. This is the true passive income part Ś you do the work once and get paid repeatedly.

The Compound Effect

Your first blog post might earn $5/month. But by the time you have 50 posts, each earning $5¢$50/month, you're looking at $500¢$2,000/month from content you wrote months ago. Affiliate marketing rewards consistency over time Ś the longer you stick with it, the more it snowballs.
