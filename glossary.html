📖 Glossary of Terms

Every term you'll encounter in the sweepstakes casino and daily collection world, explained simply.

B C D F G H K M N P R S T V W

B

Bonus Collection Software
Automation tools (like Sweeps-App) that log into multiple sweepstakes sites and claim your daily bonuses automatically. Saves 1–2 hours per day. See our software reviews.
Bounty Board
An affiliate system where you earn commissions for referring new players to sweepstakes sites. Available through affiliate networks like SweepsEdge or Routy.

C

Churning
Also: Package Churning, Welcome Package Churning
The strategy of purchasing welcome packages at sweepstakes casinos (which offer bonus SC at 150–300%), playing through the wagering requirement on high-RTP games, and cashing out the profit. The core income strategy beyond daily bonuses.
Example: Buy $10 package → get 30 SC (300% bonus) → play through at 97% RTP → cash out ~$29 → $19 profit.
Credit Card Churning
A separate strategy of opening credit cards specifically for their sign-up bonuses ($500–$750+), meeting the minimum spend requirement (often using sweepstakes purchases), then moving on to the next card. See our credit card churning guide.
Coinback / Cashback
A promotion where a site returns a percentage of your losses as SC or GC. Some sites offer daily or weekly coinback, especially on Fridays. Useful for reducing risk during playthrough.

D

Daily Collection Bonus
Also: Daily Reward, Daily SC, Login Reward
Free Sweeps Coins given to players who log in each day. The foundation of the daily collection side hustle. Amounts range from $0.05 to $5+ per site per day. Across 87+ sites, this adds up to ~$47/day.
Daily Collection Site
Also: Sweepstakes Casino, Social Casino
Our term for sweepstakes casinos — online gaming platforms that give you free coins daily. We use "daily collection site" because it's more accurate and ad-friendly than "casino." See our complete database.
Dual-Currency Model
The legal framework that makes sweepstakes casinos work. Sites offer two currencies: Gold Coins (for fun, no cash value) and Sweeps Coins (redeemable for prizes). You buy GC and receive SC as a free bonus, keeping it legal under sweepstakes law.

F

Farm w/ Gold
Whether playing with Gold Coins contributes to VIP progression on a given site. Sites marked "Farm w/ Gold ✅" let you level up VIP tiers through free play alone. Sites marked "❌" require purchases to progress.
Free Spins
Bonus spins on slot games given as part of daily rewards, promotions, or welcome packages. Usually played on specific slot titles chosen by the site.

G

GC (Gold Coins)
Also: Gold, GC Balance
The "fun" currency at sweepstakes casinos. Gold Coins have no real cash value and cannot be redeemed. They're used for playing games for entertainment. Some sites let you farm VIP levels through GC play.
God Tier
Our top-ranked category for sweepstakes sites. God Tier sites have the best daily bonuses, strongest VIP programs, lowest redemption minimums, and best overall value. These are the sites you should sign up for first.

H

High-RTP Game
A game with a high Return to Player percentage (96%+). Playing these during wagering/playthrough minimizes losses. Examples: Dice at 98% RTP, Blackjack at 97–99%, certain slot titles at 96%+.
Happy Hour
Time-limited promotions where sites boost daily rewards, give extra SC, or offer enhanced packages. Vary by site — WOW Vegas was known for Happy Hours (though they've since reduced them).

K

KYC (Know Your Customer)
Also: Identity Verification, ID Check
Identity verification required before you can redeem SC for cash. You'll typically need a government-issued photo ID and proof of address. It's a legal requirement — do it early so there's no delay when you're ready to cash out. See FAQ for details.

M

Matched Betting
A strategy for extracting guaranteed profit from sportsbook sign-up bonuses by placing opposing bets to cover all outcomes. Not gambling — it's exploiting the promotional system. Typically yields $500–$1,500 from sign-up offers. See our matched betting guide.
Minimum Redemption
Also: Min Redeem, Cash Redeem Minimum
The minimum SC amount required before you can cash out. Ranges from $20 (ReBet) to $100+ (most sites). Gift card redemptions usually have lower minimums ($10–$25).

N

No Purchase Necessary (NPN)
A legal requirement for sweepstakes. All sites must provide a free way to obtain Sweeps Coins (daily collections, mail-in requests, social media giveaways). This is what distinguishes them from gambling sites.

P

Playthrough Requirement
Also: Wagering Requirement, Rollover
The number of times you must wager SC before it becomes redeemable. A "1x playthrough" on 10 SC means you must place $10 in total bets. Higher playthrough (3x, 10x) means more wagering needed — always check before buying packages.
Example: 3x playthrough on 30 SC = must wager $90 total. At 97% RTP, you'd expect to keep ~$87 of the original $90 wagered.
Package / Coin Package
Purchasable bundles of Gold Coins that come with bonus Sweeps Coins. Welcome packages typically offer 150–300% bonus SC. The primary vehicle for the churning strategy.

R

Rakeback
A percentage of your wagering returned to you as a reward, regardless of whether you won or lost. Common in VIP programs (especially Stake.us). Higher VIP levels = higher rakeback percentage.
Redemption
The process of converting your Sweeps Coins into real money. Methods include bank transfer, PayPal, Skrill, cryptocurrency, and gift cards. Processing time is usually 1–5 business days.
RSA (Reverse Stock Arbitrage)
A stock trading strategy that exploits broker rounding policies when stocks reverse-split below $1. Can generate 900–5,600% returns per trade. Automated through tools like Greenseed. See our RSA guide.
RTP (Return to Player)
The theoretical percentage of wagered money a game pays back over time. A 97% RTP game returns $97 for every $100 wagered on average. Higher RTP = less loss during playthrough. Always choose 96%+ RTP games for churning.

S

SC (Sweeps Coins)
Also: Sweep Coins, Sweepstakes Coins
The redeemable currency at sweepstakes casinos. SC can be converted to real cash once you meet the minimum redemption amount and any playthrough requirements. 1 SC = approximately $1 in real value.
Skrill
An e-wallet payment method accepted by some sweepstakes sites with 0% transaction fees. The True Skriller cashback program adds additional savings. See our Skrill-eligible sites list.
Streak Bonus
Enhanced daily rewards for logging in on consecutive days. Many sites offer 7-day streak calendars where Day 7 gives a bigger reward (often free spins or bonus SC). Missing a day resets the streak.
Sweepstakes Casino
Also: Social Casino, Daily Collection Site
An online gaming platform that uses a dual-currency sweepstakes model (GC + SC) to operate legally in most US states. Players can earn and redeem Sweeps Coins for real cash prizes without it being classified as gambling.

T

Tier System
Our ranking system for sweepstakes sites: God Tier (best), High Tier, Medium Tier, and New Tier (recently added, under evaluation). Based on daily bonuses, VIP programs, redemption minimums, and community feedback.

V

VIP Program
Also: Loyalty Program, VIP Tiers
A rewards system where your activity (purchases, play volume) unlocks progressively better benefits — higher daily bonuses, exclusive packages, faster redemptions, rakeback, and personal account managers. Some sites let you progress through free Gold Coin play.
VIP Transfer
A program offered by some sites (like McLuck) where you can transfer your VIP status from a competitor. Often comes with bonus SC and instant VIP perks. A great way to skip the grind on a new site.

W

Wagering Requirement
Also: Playthrough, Rollover Requirement
See Playthrough Requirement. The total amount you must bet before SC becomes redeemable. Lower is better — 1x is standard for daily bonuses, 1x–3x is typical for welcome packages.
Welcome Package
Also: Welcome Bonus, First Purchase Bonus
A special offer for new players, typically giving 150–300% bonus SC on your first purchase(s). The most profitable packages to churn because the bonus percentage is highest. Some sites offer incremental welcome packages across your first 3–5 purchases.
Example: "$10 for 30 SC (300%)" means you pay $10 and receive 30 SC instead of the usual 10.
