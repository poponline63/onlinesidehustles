📈 Strategy Breakdown

🎰 Daily Login Collection (Free)

The foundation. Log into sweepstakes sites daily and claim free SC. No money needed, no risk. With 87+ sites tracked, the ceiling is ~$47/day or $1,400/month. Even hitting 30 sites gets you $450+/month for 15–20 minutes of work (or less with automation).

$450–$1,400/month

💸 Welcome Package Churning

Buy welcome packages (150–300% bonus), play through on 96–98% RTP games, cash out profit. Average profit per package: $15–$25. With 50+ sites offering packages, this is a renewable monthly income source. Requires some capital but ROI is consistently positive. Read our full churning guide.

$500–$2,000/month

⚽ Matched Betting

Extract guaranteed profit from sportsbook sign-up bonuses by covering all outcomes. Not gambling — it's math. Initial sign-up bonuses yield $1,000–$3,000 total. Ongoing reload offers add $200–$500/month. Read our matched betting guide.

$500–$1,500/month (ongoing)

📈 RSA (Reverse Stock Arbitrage)

Exploit broker rounding when stocks reverse-split below $1. Returns of 900–5,600% per trade. Automated with Greenseed (code "CROSSROADS"). Requires brokerage accounts and understanding the mechanics. Power users report $400+/day. Read our RSA guide.

$1,000–$5,000/month

💳 Credit Card Churning

Open credit cards for $500–$750+ sign-up bonuses, meet minimum spend through sweepstakes purchases (double dipping!), close/downgrade before annual fees. 2–3 cards per month = $1,000–$2,000+. See our credit card churning guide.

$500–$2,000/month

🎁 Reward Apps (Passive)

Stack cashback and reward apps for money you'd spend anyway. FreeCash, Rakuten, Ibotta, Fetch Rewards, Swagbucks, Honeygain, Coin. Minimal effort, runs in the background. See our reward apps guide.

$50–$300/month
