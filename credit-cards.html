Match your spending habits to the best rewards structure

🍔

Heavy on Groceries & Dining?

Go with the Amex Gold (4X on both) or Blue Cash Preferred (6% at supermarkets). If you spend $500+/month on food, these cards pay for themselves.

✈️

Frequent Traveler?

The Amex Platinum and Chase Sapphire Reserve give you lounge access, travel credits, and massive point multipliers. Premium perks offset the annual fees fast.

💰

Want Maximum Value, Low Cost?

The Chase Sapphire Preferred ($95/yr) is the sweet spot — 75K bonus points, 5x on travel, 3x on dining. Best bang for the buck.

💲

No Annual Fee? No Problem.

The Chase Freedom Unlimited has $0 annual fee, 0% intro APR for 15 months, and still earns 1.5-5% on everything. Perfect starter card.
