What is Hello Millions?

Hello Millions is a B2 platform (same company as McLuck) that launched in 2023. It occupies a similar space to McLuck but without the VIP transfer perk that makes McLuck more attractive.

The game library has around 400+ titles with a decent mix of slots and table games. The daily bonus recently switched to a wheel spin format, giving $0.20-$0.40 SC per day. It's not thrilling, but it's slightly better than the old flat rate.

Hello Millions is fine. It's legit, it pays out, and the welcome offers are decent. But with McLuck offering better dailies and VIP perks from the same company, Hello Millions feels like the B-team. Still worth adding to your rotation if you're maximizing daily collections.
