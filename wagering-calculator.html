How this calculator works

Every figure updates instantly as you type. Using a $100 package that gives 130 SC as the example (1 SC redeems for $1):

  • Bonus (free SC) = SC received − price = 130 − 100 = 30 SC. That's your built-in edge before playthrough.
  • Total you must wager = SC received × playthrough multiplier. At 1x, you wager 130 SC before redeeming.
  • House edge = 100% − the game's RTP. A 99% RTP game has a 1% edge against you.
  • Expected loss = total wagered × house edge = 130 × 1% = 1.30 SC given back while clearing.
  • SC left to redeem = SC received − expected loss = 128.70 SC.
  • Net profit = SC left − price = 128.70 − 100 = $28.70 (a 28.7% return).

The takeaway: a bigger bonus, low playthrough, and high-RTP games = more profit kept. This is the core of profitable package churning.

Why high-RTP games matter

Clearing a bonus on a 96% slot instead of a 99.5% blackjack table can cost you several times more in expected loss for the same bonus. That gap is exactly where churners win or lose. Always clear playthrough on the highest-RTP game a site allows.
