Sweepstake casinos cleverly operate under promotional sweepstakes laws instead of traditional gambling regulations, which exempts them from paying casino taxes. By distributing free SC (Social Coins, where 1SC = $1) and offering bonus packages, they qualify as sweepstakes rather than gambling establishments. The tax savings from this structure far exceed the cost of giving away free coins and bonuses.

Think of it like retail stores asking customers to donate to charity at checkout - the business gets the tax write-off. Similarly, when you claim free SC, you're helping these casinos maintain their tax-advantaged sweepstakes status, saving them millions in casino taxes annually.

These platforms offer free SC through daily collections, promotional links, and bonus packages, betting that most users will lose it back through gameplay.
