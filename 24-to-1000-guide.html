🎰 How Sweepstakes Casinos Work

Sweepstakes casinos operate under promotional sweepstakes laws instead of traditional gambling regulations, which exempts them from paying casino taxes. By distributing free SC (Social Coins, where 1SC = $1) and offering bonus packages, they qualify as sweepstakes rather than gambling establishments.

Think of it like retail stores asking customers to donate to charity at checkout — the business gets the tax write-off. Similarly, when you claim free SC, you help these casinos maintain their tax-advantaged sweepstakes status, saving them millions in casino taxes annually.

These platforms offer free SC through daily collections, promotional links, and bonus packages, betting that most users will lose it back through gameplay. Their business model relies on multiple profit streams: tax exemptions, house edge on games, and users squandering their free coins. However, by understanding this system, you can flip the script and become a profitable player who consistently extracts value from their promotions.
